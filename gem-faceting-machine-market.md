<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Dual Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gem faceting machine market is primarily categorized into single-axis and dual-axis types. Single-axis machines allow for the rotation of the gemstone around one axis, making them suitable for simpler cuts and designs. In contrast, dual-axis machines enable movement along two axes, facilitating more complex faceting and precision cuts. This dual functionality enhances creativity and efficiency in gem cutting, appealing to both amateur and professional gemologists. The choice between the two depends on the intricacy of the desired gemstone design.</p></p>
