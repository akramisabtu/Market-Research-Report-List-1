<p><p>Geothermal Heat Pumps (GHPs) utilize the stable underground temperature to heat and cool buildings efficiently. The market features three main systems: Vertical Closed Loop, where pipes are drilled deep into the ground, ideal for limited space; Horizontal Closed Loop, which buries pipes in shallow trenches, suitable for larger areas; and Open Loop, using groundwater from wells for heat exchange, needing sustainable water sources. Each type offers unique benefits based on land requirements and climate.</p></p>
