<p><p>The industrial ultrafast lasers market includes various types, each with unique characteristics. Titanium-sapphire lasers are known for their high pulse energy and tunability, making them suitable for precision applications. Diode-pumped lasers offer compact designs and efficiency, ideal for industrial settings. Fiber lasers provide excellent beam quality and robustness for cutting and marking. Mode-locked diode lasers are valued for their short pulse duration and versatility in research.
