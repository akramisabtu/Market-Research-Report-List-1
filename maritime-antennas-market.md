<p><p>The maritime antennas market encompasses various types designed for specific communication and navigation needs. SSB antennas facilitate long-range communication over shortwave frequencies, essential for maritime operations. VHF antennas are crucial for short-distance voice communication and safety alerts. AIS antennas support the Automatic Identification System, enhancing vessel tracking and collision avoidance. GPS antennas enable precise positioning and navigation. Additionally, "Other" antennas include specialized devices tailored for unique maritime applications, such as radar or satellite communication, ensuring comprehensive connectivity at sea.</p></p>
