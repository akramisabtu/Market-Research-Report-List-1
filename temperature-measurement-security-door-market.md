<p><p>The temperature measurement security door market is divided into two main types: multi-channel detection and single-channel detection. Multi-channel detection systems utilize multiple sensors to assess temperature variations across various points, enhancing accuracy and providing comprehensive monitoring. In contrast, single-channel detection systems focus on a single point of measurement, offering a simpler and often cost-effective solution. Both types serve essential roles in security applications, ensuring the integrity of controlled environments by detecting unauthorized access or potential threats based on temperature anomalies.</p></p>
