<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market features key types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors detect rotational speed through magnetic fields, providing accurate readings and quick response times, making them popular in modern vehicles. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors utilize inductance changes from rotating ferromagnetic materials, offering simplicity and durability, although potentially less precision. Both types are essential for effective vehicle stability control, anti-lock braking systems, and overall vehicle safety monitoring.</p></p>
