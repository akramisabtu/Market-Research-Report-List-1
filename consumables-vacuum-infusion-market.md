<p><p>The consumables market for vacuum infusion encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Vacuum Bagging Film**: Used to create a seal over the composite materials, ensuring a controlled environment during infusion. </p><p>2. **Release Film**: Provides a barrier between the laminate and the bagging, facilitating easy part removal after curing.</p><p>3. **Peel Ply**: Applied on the surface of the laminate to ensure a clean, bondable surface for subsequent layers or paints.</p><p>4. **Breathers and Bleeders**: Assist in the flow of resin and the evacuation of air, critical for optimal infusion. </p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes various specialty products that enhance the vacuum infusion process.</p></p>
