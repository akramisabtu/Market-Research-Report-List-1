Lightweight cotton is ideal for delicate projects like baby quilts and summer garments, offering breathability and softness. Medium-weight cotton provides a balance, suitable for a variety of quilting and craft projects, ensuring durability while remaining flexible. Heavyweight cotton is robust, perfect for structured items and upholstery, delivering strength and longevity in more demanding applications. Each type serves distinct quilting needs.</p></p>
