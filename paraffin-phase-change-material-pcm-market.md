<p><p>Paraffin Phase Change Materials (PCM) are substances that absorb and release thermal energy during phase transitions, typically between solid and liquid states. The market for paraffin PCM includes several types, notably shape-stabilized PCM and microencapsulated PCM. Shape-stabilized PCM maintains its form while effectively storing heat, making it ideal for building materials. Microencapsulated PCM consists of tiny paraffin droplets encased in a protective shell, allowing for integration into various applications like textiles and coatings, enhancing thermal management and energy efficiency.</p></p>
