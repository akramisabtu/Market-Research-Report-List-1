<p><p>An Acute Hemodialysis Catheter is a medical device designed to provide vascular access for patients undergoing hemodialysis treatment, particularly in acute settings such as hospitals. <p><p>The Acute Hemodialysis Catheter market is segmented into two main types: short-term and long-term catheters. Short-term catheters are designed for temporary access, typically used in emergency situations or when immediate dialysis is required, providing quick and convenient vascular access. Long-term catheters, on the other hand, are intended for extended use, often employed in patients with chronic kidney conditions. These catheters are usually more durable and designed for prolonged patient management in a hospital or outpatient setting.</p></p>
