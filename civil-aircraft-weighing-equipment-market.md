<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform System</li><li>Jack Weigh System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment market primarily comprises two types: platform systems and jack weigh systems. Platform systems consist of large, flat scales where the entire aircraft is positioned for weighing, ensuring stability and accuracy. In contrast, jack weigh systems utilize hydraulic jacks to lift the aircraft, allowing for individual wheel weight measurement. Both systems are essential for compliance with safety standards and ensuring effective load distribution, ultimately enhancing operational efficiency in civil aviation.</p></p>
