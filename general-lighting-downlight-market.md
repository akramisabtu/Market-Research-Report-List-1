<p><p>The general lighting downlight market primarily consists of two types: surface-mounted and recessed downlights. Surface-mounted downlights are installed directly on the ceiling's surface, making them easy to install and suitable for various spaces, including homes and commercial settings. In contrast, recessed downlights are embedded into the ceiling, offering a sleek, clean appearance that enhances aesthetics and provides even, ambient lighting. Both types are integral to modern lighting design, catering to different preferences and architectural requirements.</p></p>
