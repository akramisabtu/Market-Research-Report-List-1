<p><p>The 3D Medical Imaging Services market encompasses various modalities, notably ultrasound and MRI. Ultrasound utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images, ideal for soft tissue evaluation and prenatal monitoring. In contrast, MRI employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed cross-sectional images, making it valuable for assessing brain, spine, and joint conditions. Both imaging types enhance diagnostic accuracy, improve treatment planning, and facilitate patient monitoring, driving growth in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
