<p><strong>The Cone Penetrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand Operated Static Cone Penetrometer</li><li>Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cone penetrometer market is divided into two main types: hand-operated static cone penetrometers and self-start static cone penetrometers. Hand-operated models require manual force to drive the cone into the soil, making them cost-effective and suitable for smaller projects. In contrast, self-start static cone penetrometers utilize automated systems for penetration, providing greater speed and efficiency for larger or more complex sites. Both types are essential for determining soil strength and properties in geotechnical engineering assessments.</p></p>
