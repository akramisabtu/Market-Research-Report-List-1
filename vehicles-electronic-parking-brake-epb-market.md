<p><p>The Vehicles Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) market comprises two primary types: Caliper Integrated EPB and Cable Puller EPB. Caliper Integrated EPB systems are built directly into the brake caliper, enhancing design efficiency and space utilization. They offer faster response times and improved performance. In contrast, Cable Puller EPB systems utilize traditional cables to engage the brake, providing a more mechanical approach. Both types aim to improve vehicle safety, convenience, and ease of use, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
