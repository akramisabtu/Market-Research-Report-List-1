<p><strong>The Dialyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-flux Dialyzers</li><li>Low-flux Dialyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialyzers market is primarily divided into two types: high-flux and low-flux dialyzers. High-flux dialyzers allow for the passage of larger molecules and enhance the clearance of a broader range of toxins, making them ideal for patients with advanced kidney disease. In contrast, low-flux dialyzers are designed for the removal of smaller molecules and offer a more limited filtration capacity. The choice between these types depends on the patient's specific medical needs and treatment goals.</p></p>
