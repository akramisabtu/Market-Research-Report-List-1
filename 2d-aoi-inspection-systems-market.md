<p><strong>The 2D AOI Inspection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline 2D AOI</li><li>Offline 2D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems market can be divided into two main types: inline and offline AOI. Inline 2D AOI systems are integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of PCBs during manufacturing, which enhances efficiency and reduces errors. In contrast, offline 2D AOI systems operate separately from the production line, enabling detailed inspections after assembly. Each type serves different operational needs, with inline focusing on speed and efficiency, while offline emphasizes thorough analysis and quality control.</p></p>
