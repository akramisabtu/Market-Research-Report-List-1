<p><p>The rubber vulcanizer market comprises several types, including elemental sulfur vulcanizers, peroxide vulcanizing agents, quinone derivatives, and other specialized agents. Elemental sulfur is the most common, enhancing heat resistance and elasticity. Peroxide agents, often used in high-performance applications, provide superior aging resistance. Quinone derivatives offer unique properties for specific formulations. Other vulcanizing agents may include metallic oxides and amines, catering to diverse rubber applications. Together, these agents enable the production of durable, high-quality rubber products.</p></p>
