<p><strong>The Multiple Effect Distillation (MED) Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multiple Effect Distillation with Thermal Vapour Compression (MED-TVC)</li><li>Multiple Effect Distillation with Mechanical Vapour Compression (MED-MVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multiple Effect Distillation (MED) market includes two main types: MED with Thermal Vapour Compression (MED-TVC) and MED with Mechanical Vapour Compression (MED-MVC). MED-TVC enhances efficiency by using the heat from evaporated vapour to preheat incoming feedwater, reducing energy consumption. In contrast, MED-MVC employs mechanical compressors to recycle energy from vapours, enabling lower operating pressures and energy savings. Both technologies are integral for industrial applications, particularly in desalination and chemical processing, focusing on energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
