<p><p>The smart pet trackers market is segmented into GPS-based, radio-based, and other types. GPS-based trackers use satellite signals to provide real-time location data, making them ideal for wide-ranging outdoor activities. Radio-based trackers utilize signals to track pets over shorter distances, benefiting from lower battery consumption and reduced dependency on cellular networks. Other types may include Bluetooth trackers, which connect to smartphones for nearby tracking, and advanced wearables that monitor health metrics. Each type serves different needs and preferences for pet owners.</p></p>
