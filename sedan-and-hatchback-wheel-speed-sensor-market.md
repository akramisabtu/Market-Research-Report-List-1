<p><p>In the Sedan and Hatchback Wheel Speed Sensor market, there are two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors use magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, providing precise readings essential for advanced safety systems. They are known for their reliability and durability. Conversely, Magnetic Electric sensors generate voltage signals through electromagnetic induction as the wheel rotates, offering a cost-effective solution. Both types play vital roles in vehicle stability and anti-lock braking systems, enhancing overall driving safety.</p></p>
