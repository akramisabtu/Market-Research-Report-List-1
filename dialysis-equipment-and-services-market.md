<p><strong>The Dialysis Equipment and Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Products & Supplies</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis Products & Supplies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis equipment and services market comprises two primary types: hemodialysis products and supplies, and peritoneal dialysis products and supplies. Hemodialysis involves using machines and filters, such as dialyzers and bloodlines, to cleanse blood of toxins, requiring equipment for in-clinic or home-based treatment. Peritoneal dialysis, on the other hand, utilizes a catheter and dialysate fluid to remove waste through the abdominal lining, necessitating supplies like catheters, connection systems, and sterile solutions. Both segments cater to patients with chronic kidney disease.</p></p>
