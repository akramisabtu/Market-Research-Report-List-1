<p><p>The vascular graft market comprises various types of materials used for surgical procedures to bypass or repair blood vessels. ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) vascular grafts are known for their biocompatibility and non-thrombogenic properties, making them popular for peripheral and cardiovascular applications. Polyester vascular grafts offer good tensile strength and flexibility, suitable for both arterial and venous uses. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) grafts are durable and resistant to kinking. Other graft types may include biological grafts or hybrid materials used for specific clinical needs.</p></p>
